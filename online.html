Google Virtual Day @Datostada will happen on March 12 starting at 10:00 AM (GMT-6). This day brings together different experts from different teams working at Google in AI solutions, products and research.



Schedule:

    Optimization Models in Practice: Industry-Specific Topics in Statistical Modeling.

    Introduction to Privacy.

  • 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Panel (all Googlers), Google.

    ¿Cómo llegué a IA?

  • 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Benjamín Sánchez-Lengeling, Research Scientist @Google Brain.

    Machine Learning for Scent: Learning Generalizable Perceptual Representations of Small Molecules.

  • 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Montserrat González, Research Engineer @Google AI.

    Robotics: Where are we? Myths, challenges and promises of reinforcement learning for real world robots.

  • 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Mario Guajardo, Software Engineer @Google AI.

    Best Practices for AI Product Decisions.
