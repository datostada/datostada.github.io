About Datostada

It is a free international meeting for society, academia, government and industry, about the role of data in several high-impact topics, and how people can take advantage from it, in order to prosecute and disrupt the scientific and technological potential in the southeast of the country and the neighboring regions.

Venue

Just half an hour away from Mérida, there will be free transportation for attendees!

Universidad Politécnica de Yucatán, Mérida, YUC

Yucatán is on the verge of becoming, as well as a center of cultural and natural tourism, a place of scientific formation with technology at the forefront. The Universidad Politécnica de Yucatán is a public institution of higher education that offers high-level and specialization programs on Data, Embedded Systems, Robotics and Engineering.
