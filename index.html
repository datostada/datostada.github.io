Venue

Just half an hour away from Mérida, we will cover transportation for attendees!

Parque Científico Tecnológico de Yucatán, Mérida, YUC

Yucatán is on the verge of becoming, as well as a center of cultural and natural tourism, a place of scientific formation with technology at the forefront. The Scientific Technology Park of Yucatan is a conceived center for the promotion of science and the development of environmental technologies and alternative energies. It currently houses renowned academic institutions including the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), the Center for Research in Mathematics (CIMAT) and the Center for Research and Advanced Studies of the National Polytechnic Institute (CINVESTAV), as well as high level laboratories and businesses related to tech development.
